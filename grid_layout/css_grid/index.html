Different Types of South Indian Dishes

Dosa

Crispy rice and lentil pancake served with chutney and sambar.

Idli

Soft steamed rice cakes served with coconut chutney.

Medu Vada

Deep-fried savory lentil doughnut with crispy texture.

Uttapam

Thick pancake topped with onions, tomatoes, and vegetables.

Pongal

A comforting rice and lentil dish with ghee and spices.

Upma

A healthy breakfast made from roasted semolina.
